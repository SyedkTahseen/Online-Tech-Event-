About

Puducherry Technological University (PTU) is a premier institution and the first state university in the Union Territory of Puducherry. Established in 2020, PTU builds on the legacy of the renowned Pondicherry Engineering College (PEC), founded in 1985. With a sprawling 185.8-acre campus on the shores of the Bay of Bengal, PTU offers a vibrant ecosystem for innovation and intellectual growth.

Techblaze is a platform dedicated to empowering individuals and organizations through cutting-edge technology education and innovation. We specialize in organizing world-class technical workshops, conferences, and hands-on training sessions that bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application.
